What we collect

When you contact us via WhatsApp, email, or any form on this site, we may collect:

  • Your name and contact details (email, phone number)
  • Information about your property or business (location, unit count, current channels)
  • Any information you choose to share when requesting an audit or quote

We do not currently use cookies or third-party analytics tools on this site. If we add them in future, this policy will be updated and a cookie banner will appear.

Why we collect it

We collect personal information for the following purposes only:

  • To respond to your enquiry
  • To prepare a free performance audit if you've requested one
  • To send you a proposal or contract if you've asked for pricing
  • To deliver our services if you become a client

Who we share it with

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Our service providers (e.g. CRM, email tools) under appropriate confidentiality agreements
  • Authorities, only where legally required

How long we keep it

We keep your information only as long as we need it for the purpose it was collected, or as required by law. If you ask us to delete your data, we will do so within 30 days unless we are legally required to retain it.

Your rights under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we correct or delete your information
  • Withdraw consent to processing at any time
  • Object to direct marketing
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator (South Africa) at inforegulator.org.za

To exercise any of these rights, email rob@occupied.co.za.

How we protect it

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted storage, access controls, and regular security reviews. No system is perfectly secure, but we take this seriously.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Material changes will be communicated by email where appropriate.
